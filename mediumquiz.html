How to play this game?

Globe Explorer is a game about the world, whether through its shape, flags, or facts.

There are three (3) difficulties, Easy, Medium, and Hard.

The Easy difficulty contains easy questions.
(The world's largest country by land area: Russia)

The Medium difficulty contains medium questions.
(The only country the Canada borders: United States of America)

And the Hard difficulty contains hard questions.
(The most round country: Sierra Leone)

Questions may include flags, languages, their names, and anything related.

Take note, the quizzes go on a sequential navigation, which means after you submit your answer, you cannot go back to that said question.
